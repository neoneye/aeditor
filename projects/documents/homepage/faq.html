Frequently Asked Questions

What is AEditor?

AEditor is an editor-widget specialized for programming. This widget is suppose to be embedded into programmers-editors and IDE's. It is a rival to Scintilla.

What is the goal for AEditor?

Become the greatest tool for pair-programming.

How does AEditor differ from Scintilla?

AEditor is being developed using unittesting. More test-code than implementation-code. Scintilla is not.
AEditor is written in Ruby and is therefore born with strong scripting capabilities. Scintilla is C++.
AEditor is alpha and is lacking features! Scintilla is mature.
AEditor is a small project. Less than 10000 lines of code. Scintilla is huge. More than 25000 lines of code.
AEditor aims only at being a programmers-editor-widget. Scintilla aims to be a rich-edit-widget.

What license?

BSD license.

Why is it called AEditor?

The name Æditor has some nice properties: its a gentle paraphrase of "editor" and its pronounced in danish in exacly the same way.

Must write "aeditor" when refering to Æditor.

How to help?

AEditor can always use your help. Please volunteer :-)

  • Try install AEditor, see if things is working the way you expect it to work. Report back the problems you eventually had and your thoughts on improvement. Please report back even though you don't find any problems!
  • Spend a few minutes filling out the survey's. Suggestions for questions is welcome.
  • Discussing AEditor on this forum, spawns new ideas. Pleas contribute with a few words. Your opinion matters, therefore your thoughts is vital to AEditor.
  • You are welcome to submit feature requests.
  • If you observe things which is broken, then please submit a bug report.
  • Testers is needed for: windows, linux, macos. Does it work as its suppose to on these platforms. I use FreeBSD, so I don't know? If it works or is malfunctioning then please tell.
  • Ruby-hackers is needed for the Ruby-Backend. Do you love Ruby and do you have interest in AEditor, then AEditor needs you.
  • C++hackers is needed for the C++frontend. Do you want to try writting a custom GUI-widget? Fox, WxWindows, Gtk, Qt, Cocoa, Windows?
  • Submit testcases: If you feel that AEditor should behave differnt in a paticular case, then submit some testcases and I will look at it.
  • Do you have visonary ideas about how pair-programming should be pratisized or thoughs about how an editor *should* work. Then please contribute with your insight.
